Fruit Matching is a HTML5 Matching Game. Play this coloured match-3 game with fruit to slice! You have 1 minute of time to collect as many points as possible! 3 Power-ups available:

  • Bomb: destroy fruits around (you got it if you make a combo 4).
  • Changing Fruit: destroy all fruit of a kind present in the grid (you got it if you make a combo 5).
  • Hourglass: Earn some precious seconds.
